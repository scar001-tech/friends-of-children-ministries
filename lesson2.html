Jesus Loves the Little Children

Scripture: Mark 10:13-16 Date: November 24, 2025

Lesson Sections

Objectives & Overview Lesson Content Discussions Media Resources

Objectives & Overview

Overview

This heartwarming lesson introduces learners to Jesus' special love for children. Through engaging storytelling, interactive songs, and hands-on crafts, participants will discover how precious they are to Jesus. The lesson emphasizes God's unconditional love and helps build a foundation of faith and confidence in God's care.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand that Jesus loves all children unconditionally
  • Recognize they are special and valuable to God
  • Learn to approach Jesus with confidence and trust
  • Share God's love with others

Lesson Content

Lesson Outline

Welcome Song & Opening (5 minutes)

Start the class by greeting each child by name with a warm welcome. Gather together to sing "Jesus Loves Me," encouraging everyone to participate with simple hand motions. This creates a joyful and loving atmosphere to begin the lesson.

Bible Story Time (10 minutes)

Read the story from Mark 10:13-16 using a children's Bible or tell it in simple language. Explain how people brought their children to see Jesus, but the disciples tried to send them away, thinking Jesus was too busy. Share Jesus' loving response: "Let the little children come to me! Don't stop them!" Describe how He welcomed them, blessed them, and showed them His great love.

Interactive Discussion (5 minutes)

Engage the children with simple questions to help them connect with the story. Ask them how they think the children felt when they met Jesus, what Jesus said about them, and how it makes them feel to know that Jesus loves them personally.

Craft Activity - "Jesus Loves Me" Heart (15 minutes)

Organize a craft session where children create heart-shaped cards with the message "Jesus Loves Me!" Provide supplies for them to decorate their hearts with stickers, stamps, and drawings. Help them write or trace their names on the hearts, allowing them to take these special creations home as a reminder of Jesus' love.

Action Song & Movement (5 minutes)

Get everyone moving with the song "Jesus Loves the Little Children." Encourage the children to stand up and move to the music, celebrating the beautiful truth that God loves children of all backgrounds and colors meaningful ways.

Closing Prayer (5 minutes)

Conclude the lesson with a simple prayer that the children can repeat after you. Guide them to thank Jesus for loving them, for always caring, and for making them feel special.

Discussion Questions

Q: Who tried to stop the children from seeing Jesus?
Q: What did Jesus say to the disciples?
Q: How did Jesus show His love to the children?
Q: What makes you feel loved by Jesus?
